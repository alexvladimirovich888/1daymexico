Guidelines & Safety

  • All interviews are conducted with informed consent and respect for participants' privacy.
  • The stream does not glamorize violence or criminal activity; the focus is on human stories and context.
  • Safety is a top priority: the reporter follows local guidance, avoids risky situations, and will not put anyone at risk for content.
  • Harassment, hate, or attempts to solicit illegal actions via chat are strictly prohibited.

Community Participation

Viewers can suggest respectful questions or topics ahead of time. Selected suggestions that don't endanger participants may be used during interviews. The community helps amplify local voices.

Transparency & Ethics

All donations and support go to community projects and journalistic expenses. We commit to transparency reports and to ethical reporting standards. If anyone requests removal of footage, we will comply promptly.

Resources
